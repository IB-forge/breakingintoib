About the Founder

Sophia Carter

Founder of FinancePrep

Sophia Carter, a seasoned professional with over a decade of experience in investment banking, founded FinancePrep to address the gaps she observed in traditional IB preparation. Her journey began at a top-tier financial institution, where she quickly rose through the ranks, gaining extensive experience in mergers and acquisitions, capital markets, and financial analysis. Throughout her career, Sophia mentored numerous junior colleagues and interns, realizing the need for a more practical and accessible approach to IB training. This realization led her to create FinancePrep, a platform designed to provide aspiring investment bankers with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in the competitive world of finance. Sophia's vision is to democratize access to high-quality IB preparation, ensuring that talent from all backgrounds has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Mission

At FinancePrep, our mission is to empower aspiring investment bankers with the knowledge and skills they need to excel in their careers. We are committed to providing clear, concise, and practical guidance that bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world application. Our courses are designed to be accessible to students and early professionals worldwide, regardless of their background or location. We believe that with the right preparation, anyone can achieve their goals in the competitive field of investment banking. We strive to create a supportive community where learners can connect, share insights, and grow together.

If you've found FinancePrep helpful, please share it with your network to support others on their IB journey.
